About Us

About EVOLVE, Events and Promotions

Creative, reliable, and built to make brands stand out.

For over 8 years, Sandeep K D has been leading EVOLVE, Events and Promotions, a trusted event management company that partners with premium brands and organizations to deliver unique and memorable experiences.

Sandeep and his team specialize in fundraising event planning and, with help from a strong professional network, have organized many events that support charities and non-profit organizations. He believes in working only with the best — from photographers and designers to artists and promoters — so every event is managed with creativity, professionalism, and calm under pressure.

Beyond EVOLVE, Sandeep is a marketing strategist and an experienced event planner, known for organising some of India’s most celebrated film award nights. He has contributed to major events like the Dubai Marathon, International Yoga Day in Mumbai, and the Thakare Foundation. He is also a founding member of The Spoke Club and the Young Patrons Council for the Royal Dubai Museum.

What we do

At EVOLVE, Events and Promotions we bring brands closer to people through creative, high-impact activity. We deliver eye-catching vehicle branding such as bus displays, bike rallies, canter activities and cartoon-character promotions. Our promoter services include leaflet distribution, paper insertions, food sampling, manpower supply, crowd engagement and door-to-door campaigns to boost reach and conversions. We also provide complete branding solutions — flex and office branding, hoardings, sign boards, auto branding, GSB boards and store inaugurations — so your brand stands out wherever it appears.
